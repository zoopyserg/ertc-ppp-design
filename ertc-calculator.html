Employee Retention Tax Credit Calculator

The purpose of this calculator is to provide a preliminary determination of your eligibility for the ERTC credits, and then estimate the refund that you should expect upon a more thorough and detailed computation.

The precision of our estimate is limited by the precision of your inputs. You are welcome to guess-timate, but more data in = better data out.

Upon submission of your inputs, you will receive an email explaining the methodology of our calculation and how you can take immediate steps to claim your refund check.
